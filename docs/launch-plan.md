# Launch Plan

## Beta scope

- Closed beta with journalists, civic organizations, analysts, and legal reviewers.
- Mandatory accessibility pass on mobile, keyboard navigation, contrast, and form labels before public launch.
- Weekly review cadence for moderation backlog, privacy requests, and payment exceptions.

## Release gates

- CI green for lint, typecheck, build, Prisma validation, and AI worker compilation.
- Swedish legal review completed for privacy flows, publication boundaries, and payment method disclosures.
- Payment method copy verified so cash discount of 50% and crypto discount of 25% are explicit and not misleading.
- OpenAPI and public API scopes reviewed for lawful data exposure.

## Feedback collection

- Use /beta for structured feedback intake.
- Tag feedback as beta, UX, bug, or legal.
- Review low ratings and legal feedback first.

## Press kit outline

- One-page product summary explaining public-interest use case and legal guardrails.
- Founder or spokesperson bio and press contact.
- Three screenshots: dashboard, privacy center, admin legal review.
- FAQ for moderation, privacy, and monetization.

## Marketing materials

- Launch page copy for journalists and civic organizations.
- Short demo script for API access, privacy controls, and moderation workflow.
- Release notes synced with CHANGELOG.md.
